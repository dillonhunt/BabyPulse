Help & Support

Get help with BabyPulse

Getting Started

How do I add my baby?

When you first open BabyPulse, you'll see the onboarding screen. Enter your baby's name and date of birth, then tap Continue. You can add a profile photo later in Settings.

How do I log activities?

Tap the "Log" tab at the bottom. Select Feeding, Diaper, Sleep, or Pumping at the top, then choose the specific action. For breast feeding, tap Left or Right to start a timer.

Apple Watch

Does the Watch app work without my iPhone?

Yes! BabyPulse works completely independently on Apple Watch. Log feedings, diapers, and sleep even when your iPhone is off. Data syncs automatically when devices reconnect.

How do I log from my Watch?

Open BabyPulse on your Watch. The main screen shows quick-action buttons. Tap to log instantly. Swipe left/right to see today's stats and recent log.

Partner Sync

How do I share with my partner?

Go to Settings → Cloud Sync → Create Account. You'll get a 6-digit code. Share this code with your partner. They tap "Join with Code" and enter your code.

Why isn't partner sync working?

Make sure both devices have an internet connection. Partner sync requires BabyPulse+ ($4.99 one-time purchase). Check that you're both using the same share code.

Features

How do I set feeding reminders?

Go to Settings → Reminders. Toggle on "Feeding Reminders" and choose your interval (1-4 hours). Reminders automatically reset after each feeding or pumping session.

How do I export data for my doctor?

Go to Settings → Export to PDF. Choose a date range (7, 14, or 30 days, or custom). Tap Generate, then share via text, email, or AirDrop.

How do I track growth measurements?

Go to Settings → Growth Tracking. Tap + to add a new measurement. Enter weight (pounds and ounces), length, and head circumference. All measurements are saved with dates.

BabyPulse+

What's included in BabyPulse+?

BabyPulse+ is a one-time $4.99 purchase that unlocks: Full timeline history, Apple Watch complications, Weekly summary and trends, Smart feeding reminders, PDF export, Theme customization, and Cloud backup and sync.

Is it a subscription?

No! BabyPulse+ is a one-time purchase. Pay once ($4.99), use forever. No monthly fees, no annual renewals, no auto-charges.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → BabyPulse+ → Restore Purchase. This works on all devices signed in to your Apple ID at no additional cost.

Troubleshooting

Why isn't my data syncing to my Watch?

BabyPulse uses App Groups for instant sync. Make sure both iPhone and Watch are paired. If data still doesn't sync, try closing and reopening the app on both devices.

I lost my data. Can I recover it?

If you have BabyPulse+ and enabled Cloud Sync, your data is backed up to Firebase. Sign back in with your share code to restore. Without Cloud Sync, data is stored locally and can't be recovered after deletion.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Cloud Sync → Unlink Account. This removes your cloud data. To delete all local data, delete the app from your device.

Privacy & Security

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device. Optional cloud sync uses anonymous authentication—no email, phone, or name required. We don't sell data, use analytics, or track behavior. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Who can see my data?

Only you and your partner (if you share your code). Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never access your data.
