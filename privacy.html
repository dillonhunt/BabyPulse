Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

BabyPulse ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the BabyPulse mobile application.

Information We Collect

Health & Fitness Data: We collect baby care tracking data that you enter into the app, including:

  • Feeding logs (type, duration, side, amount)
  • Sleep sessions (start time, duration, quality)
  • Diaper changes (type, time)
  • Pumping sessions (duration, side, amount)
  • Growth measurements (weight, length, head circumference)

User Identifiers: When you opt to use partner sync features, we collect:

  • Anonymous Firebase authentication ID
  • Randomly generated 6-digit share code

We do NOT collect: names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, payment information, or any personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for app functionality:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Optional Cloud Sync: If you enable partner sync, your baby care data is stored in Firebase Firestore to sync between your devices and your partner's devices
  • No Analytics: We do not collect analytics, usage data, or behavioral tracking
  • No Advertising: We do not use your data for advertising purposes

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties. Your data is:

  • Stored on your device
  • Optionally synced to Firebase (a Google Cloud service) for partner sharing only
  • Never accessed by us or any third party for marketing, analytics, or any other purpose

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Data stored in Firebase is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Anonymous authentication ensures your identity is not linked to your data
  • Firebase security rules prevent unauthorized access

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Export: Use the PDF export feature to download your data
  • Delete: Deleting the app removes all local data. Cloud-synced data can be deleted by unlinking your account in Settings
  • Opt-Out: Cloud sync is optional and can be disabled at any time

Children's Privacy

BabyPulse is designed for parents to track their baby's care activities. The app collects baby care data (feedings, sleep, etc.) but does not collect any personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

BabyPulse uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For optional cloud sync and anonymous authentication. Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics are DISABLED.
  • Apple StoreKit: For in-app purchase processing. Apple handles all payment data; we never see your payment information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under CCPA. Since we do not sell personal information and collect minimal anonymous data, most CCPA provisions do not apply. However, you have the right to request deletion of your data at any time.
